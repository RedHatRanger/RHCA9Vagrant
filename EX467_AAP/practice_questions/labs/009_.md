## **Lab 9: Backup and Recovery**

### Objective
Backup and restore AAP components.

### Steps
1. **Backup Controller**:
   - Use the `ansible-callbacks` CLI to back up configurations.

2. **Backup PAH**:
   - Archive PAH data and database dumps.

3. **Simulate Restore**:
   - Restore the Controller and PAH from backups.
